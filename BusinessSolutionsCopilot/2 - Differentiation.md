
# How DU IT Consulting Differs from Large IT Service Companies & System Integrators

Below is a clear, confident, ready-to-use explanation you can share with customers.

---

## 1. One-Liner Differentiation

**“We are a specialist consulting firm, not a large IT factory. Customers choose us when they want senior experts, faster outcomes, and less complexity than what they get with big integrators.”**

---

## 2. Key Differences

### A) Engagement Model
- **Large SIs:** Many layers, long cycles, mixed-experience delivery.
- **DU IT Consulting:** Senior-led, hands-on teams; fewer layers → faster decisions.

**Talking point:**
> The experts you meet at the start are the same people who deliver.

---

### B) Focus vs. Breadth
- **Large SIs:** Offer everything to everyone.
- **DU IT Consulting:** Focused expertise where we go deep and deliver high impact.

**Talking point:**
> We don’t try to be everything. We focus on a few areas and do them exceptionally well.

---

### C) Speed & Flexibility
- **Large SIs:** Heavy processes and slow change management.
- **DU IT Consulting:** Lightweight, pragmatic delivery.

**Talking point:**
> We move at the speed business teams expect — not corporate speed.

---

### D) Outcome Orientation
- **Large SIs:** Optimized for billing teams and large programs.
- **DU IT Consulting:** Optimized for solving specific business problems.

**Talking point:**
> We measure success in outcomes — not in FTEs.

---

### E) Relationship Style
- **Large SIs:** Formal, contract-heavy interactions.
- **DU IT Consulting:** Direct, transparent, collaborative.

**Talking point:**
> We operate like an extension of your team.

---

### F) Complexity & Risk
- **Large SIs:** Heavy designs, big programs, higher complexity.
- **DU IT Consulting:** Simpler, iterative approach reducing risk.

**Talking point:**
> We keep things simple, start small, and scale what works.

---

## 3. Simple Analogy
**Large Integrator = Multi-speciality hospital** (slow, many steps)  
**DU IT Consulting = Specialist clinic** (fast, focused, expert-led)

---

## 4. When Customers Call Us
- When they want faster business outcomes.
- When they are stuck with long-running SI programs.
- When they need senior advisors, not junior-heavy teams.
- When they want clarity, simplicity, and practical value.

1. Senior-led delivery.  
2. Focused domain expertise. 
3. Faster and more flexible. 
4. Outcome-driven, not resource-driven. 
5. Partner mindset with low overhead.
