
# Tyre Manufacturing Industry – DU IT Consulting Talking Guide

## 1. Industry Problems
- Unplanned downtime and scrap
- Manual processes (Excel, paper)
- Poor traceability and slow root-cause analysis
- Reactive maintenance and poor asset history
- High inventory carrying cost
- Dealer management via phone/WhatsApp
- Slow, manual warranty processes
- Leadership lacks real-time MIS visibility

## 2. Why DU IT Consulting is Relevant
- Expertise in Microsoft Dynamics 365, Power Platform, Azure, and AI
- Ability to connect existing ERP/MES/Excel systems
- Quick-win approach (6–8 week pilots)
- Focus on real operational problems (downtime, scrap, visibility)
- Build dashboards, apps, workflows that plants and dealers actually use

## 3. Key Use Cases
### a) Real-Time OEE & Production Visibility
- Connect data from ERP/MES/Excel
- Power BI dashboards
- Power Apps for downtime capture

### b) Maintenance Request + Asset Health App
- QR-based logging
- Workflow tracking
- Asset failure history

### c) Quality Traceability & RCA Analytics
- Digital QC capture
- Correlate process parameters with defects
- Faster RCA, reduced scrap

### d) Dealer/Distributor Portal + Sales CRM
- Dynamics 365-based sales process
- Dealer app for orders & warranty claims
- Power BI for territory analysis

### e) Warranty Claims Workflow
- Mobile/web workflow
- Image uploads, approvals
- AI-driven pattern insights
